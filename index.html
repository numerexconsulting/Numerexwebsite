How it works

A simple 4-step process to get financial clarity fast, then maintain it with a steady cadence.

1) Quick diagnostic

We map current reporting, cash visibility, and decision needs. Then we set a realistic cadence.

2) Build the core model

Forecast + KPI framework that matches how you actually operate (revenue drivers, costs, headcount).

3) Reporting pack

Monthly management pack: KPIs, cash, P&L, variance notes, and next-step recommendations.

4) Ongoing cadence

Ongoing support: planning updates, stakeholder reporting, and decision support as needs arise.
